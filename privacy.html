Privacy Policy

How CLEAR PATH ADVIZORY handles personal data

This page explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, and how you can exercise your rights.

1. Who We Are

CLEAR PATH ADVIZORY provides tax support and advisory services in Ireland. For enquiries about this policy, contact info@clearpathadvisory.ie.

2. Personal Data We Collect

We may collect your name, email address, phone number, service enquiry details, tax-related documents you choose to provide, and website cookie preferences.

3. Why We Use Your Data

We use your information to respond to enquiries, deliver requested services, maintain records, comply with legal obligations, and improve the website where optional analytics are enabled.

4. Legal Bases

Depending on the situation, processing may rely on consent, steps requested before entering into a service relationship, performance of a contract, or compliance with legal obligations.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Information may be shared with service providers that help run the website, process contact forms, store communications, or support service delivery, but only where needed and subject to appropriate safeguards.

6. Retention

We keep personal data only for as long as necessary for the purpose it was collected, to manage client relationships, or to meet legal, accounting, or tax record obligations.

7. Your Rights

You may have rights to access your data, ask for correction, request erasure in some cases, restrict processing, object to certain processing, and request a copy of data you provided where applicable.

8. Cookies

We use necessary cookies for site operation. Optional analytics and marketing cookies are only enabled if you choose them through the cookie banner.

9. Security

We take reasonable technical and organisational steps to protect personal data from unauthorised access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

10. Contact and Complaints

If you have questions about how your data is used, contact us first. You may also contact the Irish Data Protection Commission if you believe your rights have been affected.

11. Form and Email Providers

This website package uses FormSubmit for contact form delivery by default. Before launch, review that provider’s terms and privacy information and update the form settings if you prefer another service.

12. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The current version will always be published on this page.
